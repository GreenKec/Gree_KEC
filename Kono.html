Profile

Kono Environmental Consulting (KEC PLC) is a well-organized consulting firm in Ethiopia specializing in environmental and social impact assessments, environmental management plans, performance auditing, and monitoring and evaluation. Established by Ethiopian professionals with diverse backgrounds, KEC PLC has a first-grade Environmental License and is recognized as a leading consultancy organization.

With extensive experience across various regions, including Oromia, Sidama, and Tigray, we are dedicated to delivering innovative services that support sustainable development.

Vision

To see a sustainable and conserved environment that provides full participation, dignity, and equal opportunities for all individuals and organizations.

Mission

To discover, promote, create, and deliver environmental protection solutions related to natural resource management, ecosystem protection, and social equity.

Our Services

  • Environmental and Social Impact Assessment (ESIA)
  • Environmental and Social Management Plans (ESMP)
  • Environmental Performance Auditing
  • Environmental Monitoring and Evaluation
  • Feasibility Studies
